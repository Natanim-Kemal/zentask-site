# ZenTask

Productivity, Reimagined. A comprehensive system designed to augment your cognitive throughput and keep you in the flow.

## Features

### Powerful Task Management
Organize tasks with custom Categories and Priorities.
Advanced Recurring Tasks: specific powerful recurrence options like "Every 2 weeks on Mon, Wed" or standard Daily/Weekly/Monthly/Yearly cycles.
Rich Details: Add due dates, detailed descriptions, and track completion status easily.

### Focus Mode
Distraction-Free View: A dedicated interface that shows only one top-priority task at a time to prevent overwhelm.
Built-in Mindfulness: Integrated Breathing Exercises with visual guides to help you center yourself before starting work.
Smart Queue: Automatically prioritizes your most important uncompleted tasks.

### Integrated Pomodoro Timer
Customizable Workflow: Fully configurable Work, Short Break, and Long Break durations to fit your style.
Background Notifications: Get alerted when a session ends, even if the tab is in the background.
Session Tracking: Automatically tracks your completed focus intervals.

### Insightful Analytics
Productivity Dashboard: Visualize your customized Completion Rates, Daily Streaks, and Total Active Tasks.
Trend Tracking: View weekly progress charts to see your productivity consistency over time.
Activity Breakdown: Detailed stats on tasks completed Today, This Week, and This Month.
